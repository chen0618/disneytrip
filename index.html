Disney Springs

Our Hotel: Pop Century Resort

A Value Resort with a Secret Superpower 🚡

Pop Century is one of Disney's Value Resorts — the most affordable tier. But it has a huge advantage that even pricier hotels don't: Skyliner gondola access directly to two parks!

  • 🏨

    Value Resort

    Disney's most affordable tier, but with recently renovated rooms — queen beds, split bathrooms, coffee makers

  • 🎨

    Decade Theming

    Giant icons from the 50s–90s: huge Rubik's Cubes, bowling pins, yo-yos, and more throughout the resort

  • 🚡

    Skyliner Access

    Direct gondola to EPCOT & Hollywood Studios — the "secret superpower" that makes this hotel punch above its weight

  • 🏊

    Hippy Dippy Pool

    Flower-power themed main pool plus two quieter pools. Perfect for winding down after a park day!

  • 🍔

    Everything POP

    Large food court with tons of options — burgers, pizza, pasta, salads, and a refillable mug station

Getting Around Disney

Everything is FREE for Resort Guests! 🎉

Staying at a Disney resort means you never need a rental car. Disney provides free transportation everywhere you need to go — by bus, boat, monorail, or our favorite: the Skyliner gondola!

🚍 Bus System

Complimentary buses run from Pop Century to every park and Disney Springs.

  • 🕑 Buses come every ~20 minutes
  • 📱 Track arrivals in the My Disney Experience app
  • 🌅 Goes to Magic Kingdom (no Skyliner to MK, bus is the way!)
  • ⏰ Plan 30–45 min for bus travel during rush times
  • 📆 Starts running ~45 min before park opening

🚡 Disney Skyliner

Our secret weapon! A gondola system that flies you over the resort right to EPCOT and Hollywood Studios.

  • 🏠 Walk out of Pop Century and hop right on
  • 🔀 Transfer at Caribbean Beach hub (quick & well-signed)
  • ⚡ ~15–20 min to either park including transfer
  • 👥 Each gondola holds up to 10 guests
  • ⛅ Closes during thunderstorms (buses are backup)

Days 2 & 5: Magic Kingdom 🏰

The Most Magical Place on Earth

This is THE Disney park — Cinderella Castle, Main Street U.S.A., classic rides, character meet-and-greets, and fireworks. Walking toward the castle for the first time is an unforgettable moment. We're going twice because there's so much to do!

🌝 Morning

Arrive early (rope drop!). Hit the big rides: Pirates, Space Mountain, Jungle Cruise

☀️ Afternoon

Explore the lands, meet characters. It's a Small World, Buzz Lightyear, Haunted Mansion

🌙 Evening

Slow down, grab dinner, and end the night with the FIREWORKS spectacular!

Pirates of the Caribbean

Classic boat ride through pirate scenes. Gentle and fun for ALL ages, including toddlers!

Space Mountain

Indoor roller coaster in the dark. A true classic and must-do for thrill seekers!

It's a Small World

The classic gentle boat ride. Perfect for little ones and a must-do for first-timers!

Haunted Mansion

Slow-moving "doom buggies" through a haunted house. Mildly spooky but fun for most kids 4+.

Jungle Cruise

Boat ride with animatronic animals and wonderfully corny jokes from your skipper guide.

Buzz Lightyear's Space Ranger Spin

Shoot targets with laser blasters from your moving car. Competitive and fun for everyone!

👶 Traveling with Little Ones (2.5 & 4.5 year olds)

Magic Kingdom has the most rides suitable for young kids! And for bigger rides, Rider Switch lets one parent ride while the other waits with kids, then swap without waiting in line again. That's one reason we're doing TWO Magic Kingdom days!

Day 3: Hollywood Studios 🚀

Movies, Star Wars & Toy Story Come to Life

Hollywood Studios has been completely transformed by two massive immersive lands: Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge and Toy Story Land. It's like stepping into the movies!

🌝 Morning

Galaxy's Edge first! Rise of the Resistance, Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run

☀️ Afternoon

Toy Story Land: Slinky Dog Dash, Toy Story Mania. Grab lunch at Woody's Lunch Box

🌙 Evening

Tower of Terror, Mickey's Runaway Railway, and dinner at Docking Bay 7

Rise of the Resistance

The most impressive ride Disney has EVER built. Multi-phase, trackless, jaw-dropping effects.

Slinky Dog Dash

Family coaster in Toy Story Land. Fun, not too intense — great for kids 38"+ tall!

Tower of Terror

Twilight Zone-themed drop tower. Thrilling and iconic! (40" height requirement)

Toy Story Mania!

3D shooting gallery ride. Excellent for ALL ages, including very young kids. So much fun!

Mickey & Minnie's Runaway Railway

Trackless ride inside a cartoon. No height requirement — great for the whole family!

Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run

YOU pilot the Millennium Falcon. Yes, really. Interactive cockpit — prepare for hyperspace!

🌟 Galaxy's Edge is NEXT LEVEL

You literally step onto another planet (Batuu). Cast Members stay in character, Coca-Cola comes in Star Wars containers, and the level of immersive detail is unlike anything at any other theme park in the world.

Day 4: EPCOT 🌍

Innovation, Adventure & Eating Around the World

EPCOT has two halves: the front features world-class rides and technology, and the back — World Showcase — takes you around the globe through 11 country pavilions with authentic food, architecture, and culture from each nation.

🌝 Morning

Guardians of the Galaxy: Cosmic Rewind, Test Track, Frozen Ever After

☀️ Afternoon

Stroll World Showcase — eat & drink around the world! 11 countries to explore

🌙 Evening

Spaceship Earth, Remy's Ratatouille, and the fireworks over the lagoon

Spaceship Earth

Ride inside the iconic "golf ball" through the history of human communication. Gentle and fascinating!

Guardians of the Galaxy: Cosmic Rewind

Reverse-launch indoor coaster with a rotating ride vehicle and an awesome soundtrack!

Test Track

Design your own virtual car, then ride it through tests at high speed on an outdoor track!

Frozen Ever After

Boat ride through Arendelle with Anna, Elsa, and the whole gang. The kids will be obsessed!

Remy's Ratatouille Adventure

Shrunk to rat size in a French kitchen! Trackless ride, no height requirement. All ages!

World Showcase

11 countries, authentic food, real cultural representatives. Walk 1.2 miles around the world!

🌎 Eating Around the World

An EPCOT tradition! Sample food and drinks from each of the 11 countries as you stroll the lagoon: Mexico, Norway, China, Germany, Italy, USA, Japan, Morocco, France, United Kingdom, Canada. The pastries at Les Halles in France are some of the best food on Disney property!

Rope Drop Strategy ⏰

How We Get Ahead of the Crowds Every Morning

Disney's Early Theme Park Entry lets on-property resort guests enter parks 30 minutes before the official opening. That's a huge advantage — shorter lines, cooler temps, and first dibs on the big rides. But there's a trick to making it work even better.

Standard Approach

🚍 Take the Resort Bus

Free and convenient, but you'll arrive later than the most eager guests.

  • 🕑 First buses leave Pop Century ~7:20–7:30 AM
  • 🚶 Arrive at park ~7:45–8:00 AM
  • 👥 Long lines already forming at security & tapstiles
  • 🔄 People who drove or took rideshare are already ahead of you
⭐ Our Strategy

🚕 Call a Minnie Van

Disney's own ride service (through Lyft). We call one around 6:45–6:50 AM to beat the buses!

  • 🚗 Picked up at Pop Century ~6:50 AM
  • ⚡ Arrive at the park before any buses do
  • 🏆 First in line at security & tapstiles
  • 🎭 Ride 2–3 big rides with almost no wait before regular opening!
💡 Lessons from Our January 2026 Trip

We learned this the hard way! On our first morning at MK, we took the resort bus and arrived to find huge crowds already inside the park. People who drove themselves or used rideshare had beaten us there. After that, we started calling a Minnie Van every morning at 6:45 — and it made a HUGE difference. We were walking onto rides that later had 60+ minute waits.

🚢 Secret Beignet Mission: The Free Boat to Port Orleans

You don't need to stay at Port Orleans to eat there! Take the free Sassagoula River Cruise boat from Disney Springs directly to Port Orleans French Quarter. The scenic ride takes about 17 minutes along the river — it's relaxing and beautiful. Hop off, grab beignets at Scat Cat's Cafe, and boat back. This is one of Disney World's best-kept secrets!
